Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors game!

Please follow the instructions:

  1. Open your browser's developer tools.

  2. Select the "console" menu.

  3. In front of the grey double arrow, start by typing exactly as it is: letsPlay()

  4. Once letsPlay() has been typed, press enter.

  5. On the newly generated window type either: rock, paper or scissors.

  6. The result is displayed on the console, " Your choice vs machine: item 1, item 2".

  7. item 1 is the user choice, item 2 is the randomly generated choice by the computer.

  8. To play again, make sure step 1 and 2 are in execution, and repeat every new attempt from step 3.
